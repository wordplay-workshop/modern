Overview

Date and time: November 5th-9th, 2025

Location: Suzhou, China

Shared task: Official website

Submission website: OpenReview

This workshop focused on exploring the utility of interactive narratives, think everything from classic text-adventures like Zork to modern Twine games, to fill a role as the learning environments of choice for language-based tasks including but not limited to storytelling.

We like all things:

  • Interactive narrative: game playing RL agents, game generation, etc.
  • Interactive language learning
  • Natural language generation
  • Improvisational storytelling
  • And more! Anything you can think of that involves narrative, interactivity, and language!
